About David Bowie

  • Jan 8th 1947

    David Bowie is born David Robert Hayward-Jones in Brixton, south London.

  • Dec 25th 1959

    Bowie receives his first instrument for Christmas, a plastic saxophone.

  • Jun 1st 1967

    Releases debut, self-titled rock album by Deram Records.

  • Jun 27th 1986

    Fantasy film “The Labyrinth” starring Bowie is released.

  • Jan 10th 2016

    Bowie passes just 3 days after the release of his final album Black Star.

I don’t know where I’m going from here, but I promise it won’t be boring.

- David Bowie

About Prince

  • Jun 7th 1985

    Prince is born Prince Rogers Nelson in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

  • Apr 7th 1978

    Releases debut album titled “For You” which peaked at 163 on the charts.

  • Jun 25th 1984

    Releases the album “Purple Rain” and stars in the movie of the same name.

  • Mar 15th 2004

    Inducted into the Rock & Roll Hall of Fame and performs at the Grammy’s that same year.

  • Apr 21st 2016

    At the age of 57 Prince passes in his recording studio and home in Minnesota.

Dearly beloved, we are gathered here today to get through this thing called life.

- Prince
